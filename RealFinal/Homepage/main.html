Calgary Alberta Temple Fun Fact:
Once the Temple was completed, a public open house was held from September 29 to October 20, 2012, where over 100,000 people toured the temple.

Redlands California Temple Fun Fact:
The Redlands California Temple stands on a parcel of the original Mormon landholdings purchased in October 1851 by Elders Amasa Lyman and Charles C. Rich.

Salt Lake City Temple Fun Fact:
With 23 sealing rooms, the Salt Lake Temple has more sealing rooms than any other temple.

Bountiful Utah Temple Fun Fact:
Church president Howard W. Hunter dedicated the Bountiful Utah Temple. Two hundred thousand Latter-day Saints attended the dedicatory sessions, more than had ever previously attended a temple dedication.
