WDD 230 Lesson 6 Class Activity

In week 5 you should have finished your SoloLearn JavaScript course. You will be taking these principles and concepts and applying them today.

You will create a container that will display the sum of two values.

Instructions

  • You will make a container that will display the totals of two numbers.
  • Your html file should have 2 inputs, 1 button, and a container to show your output of the two numbers.
  • Your JavaScript file should have 1 function that will run when onclick event occurs.
  • Your css file will include styles for your container and other elements.

Here it shows that 2 + 1 = 3 the container is visually full 3/10

Here it shows that 5 + 5 = 10 the container is visually full 10/10

Stretch Goals:

  • Make the container hold up to 100 or a different value of your choice.
  • You could also make a “container size” input and have that new value used in the JavaScript.
  • Add a subtraction button to subtract two values and display the outcome.
  • What could you do to do error handling for these?
  • The container cannot hold more than 10ml or it will overflow.
  • The container also cannot hold a negative amount of liquid.
  • The container cannot hold a non-number value.
