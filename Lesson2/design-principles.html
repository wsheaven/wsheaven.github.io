PARC: Contrast

  • Apple
  • The Website

    • The example above demonstrates the usefulness of using contrast in web design. They do this by having each product have a different and contrasting background color. This allows the user to quickly recognize that they are different products. It also helps draw the users eyes around the screen. To notice everything and not just one item.
